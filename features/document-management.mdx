The Document Management module in HCMatrix provides a centralized, secure repository for all HR-related documents. From employee files and contracts to company policies and compliance records, this feature ensures your important documents are organized, accessible, and secure.

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Consistent Naming**: Use clear, consistent naming conventions for files and folders (e.g., "LastName_FirstName_OfferLetter_2024.pdf").
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Regular Cleanup**: Archive or delete outdated documents regularly to keep the system organized and compliant with retention policies.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Metadata is Key**: Always add relevant metadata and tags to documents for easier searching and organization.
</Tip>

<Tip>
  **Audit Access**: Regularly review access logs and permissions to ensure only authorized users can access sensitive documents.
</Tip>

<Warning>
  **Sensitive Data**: Employee files contain highly sensitive personal information. Implement strict access controls and encryption.
</Warning>

<Warning>
  **Retention Compliance**: Follow legal retention requirements for different document types. Some documents must be kept for 7+ years.
</Warning>

## Data Retention Policies

**Required Retention Periods:**
- I-9 forms: 3 years after hire or 1 year after termination (whichever is later)
- Payroll records: 3-7 years (varies by jurisdiction)
- FMLA records: 3 years
- OSHA records: 5-30 years (depending on type)
- EEO-1 reports: 1 year
- Injury/illness records: 5 years
- Performance reviews: 3-7 years
